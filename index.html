A Bit About Me

Hello! I am Conor Daly (aka. Skyclo), a self-taught developer and high school student. When I was eight years old, I discovered my passion for technology. Since then I have learned both front-end and backend languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQL. I also have experience with TypeScript, C# and others. I have also created some awesome things like Ideoxan, node-scdl, and Calicode.

Over the years, I have used tools like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Inkscape, Unreal Engine, and Autodesk Maya. Since around 2017, I started to work on logo and UI design. I created redesign concepts for applications like Spotify and Discord. I've also done commission work.
